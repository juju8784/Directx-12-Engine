typedef forceRaw:: Events GW::AUDIO::GAudio3D::Events

Events generated by GAudio3D (only)

These messages are used to notify observers when Update3DListener is called. 

Events::UPDATE_LISTENER                     Notifies connected GMusic3D and GSound3D about updated listener matrix.
Events::UPDATE_TRANSFORM_AND_SPATIALIZE     Notifies connected GMusic3D and GSound3D about new transform and Spatialize call.
